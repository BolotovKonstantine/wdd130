Russia

Russia, officially known as the Russian Federation, is the largest country in the world by land area, spanning Eastern Europe and northern Asia. It boasts a diverse range of environments, from the frozen tundra of Siberia to the subtropical beaches of the Black Sea coast. Russia has a rich historical tapestry that includes the reign of the tsars, the rise and fall of the Soviet Union, and its significant role on the global stage as a major political and economic power. Its cultural heritage is equally vast, encompassing world-renowned contributions to literature, music, and the arts
